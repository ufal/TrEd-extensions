BINDINGS

'BackSpace'

Annotate: Undo recent annotation action invoke_undo

'CTRL+End'

Move to Leftmost Descendant move_deep_end

'CTRL+F1'

Show / Hide Morphological Tags 1 toggle_tag_1

'CTRL+F2'

Show / Hide Morphological Tags 2 toggle_tag_2

'CTRL+F3'

Show / Hide Morphological Tags 3 toggle_tag_3

'CTRL+Home'

Move to Rightmost Descendant move_deep_home

'CTRL+Return'

ThisAddress() to Clipboard ThisAddressClipBoard

'CTRL+SHIFT+Down'

Move Down to Fork move_to_fork

'CTRL+SHIFT+Up'

Move Up to Root move_to_root

'CTRL+equal'

Toggle Children Hiding tree_hide_mode

'CTRL+j'

Toggle Tree Justify Mode tree_justify_mode

'CTRL+l'

Display Trees Left-to-Right direction_LTR

'CTRL+minus'

Hide Children Subtrees hide_children

'CTRL+plus'

Unhide Children Recursively unhide_subtree

'CTRL+r'

Display Trees Right-to-Left direction_RTL

'CTRL+underscore'

Hide This Subtree hide_this

'End'

Move to Last Word move_word_end

'Home'

Move to First Word move_word_home

'SHIFT+End'

Move to Last Paragraph move_par_end

'SHIFT+Home'

Move to First Paragraph move_par_home

'SHIFT+Next'

Move to Next Paragraph move_to_next_paragraph

'SHIFT+Prior'

Move to Prev Paragraph move_to_prev_paragraph

'exclam'

Annotate: Edit annotator's comment edit_comment
